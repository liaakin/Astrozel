Element Distribution

In the general birth chart analysis at Astrozel, we also focus on the fascinating world of elements – Fire, Earth, Air, and Water. These elements are essential building blocks that shape the personality of each individual. The element distribution provides a holistic view of the energetic structure of your horoscope. It refers to the distribution of planets in the astrological signs, which are associated with the four elements. Each element has distinct characteristics, and how these are distributed in your chart can influence various life aspects such as core personality traits and behaviors, relationships, career and professional preferences, emotional stability, and resilience. A balanced element ratio promotes a harmonious way of life, while an over- or under-emphasis of an element may indicate challenges that can be addressed through conscious adjustments and personal growth. This analysis examines whether there is an over- or under-emphasis of any element and what effects this may have.

Synastry Analysis

A synastry analysis is an astrological method that involves comparing the horoscopes of two individuals to understand the dynamics of their relationship. This includes studying the positions of the planets, houses, and aspects in the individual horoscopes, as well as their interactions. The synastry analysis can provide insights into the strengths, challenges, and potential of a relationship by illuminating mutual attraction, communication, conflicts, and the emotional connection between the individuals. Questions that can be answered through a synastry analysis include:
  • What are the main themes and dynamics in the relationship?
  • Which areas could potentially cause conflicts or tensions?
  • How can the individuals communicate and support each other better?
  • What types of activities or interests could strengthen the relationship?
  • Are there potential long-term prospects for the relationship?

Composite Chart Analysis

A composite chart analysis, on the other hand, refers to the calculation of an average horoscope created from the mean positions of the planets of both individuals. This analysis views the relationship as an independent entity with its own identity and dynamics. It reveals the common goals, values, and challenges. Questions that can be answered through a composite chart analysis include:
  • What are the fundamental goals and purposes of the relationship?
  • How can the individuals work together more effectively to achieve their goals?
  • What strengths and resources does the relationship bring?
  • What obstacles could impair the relationship, and how can they be overcome?
  • How can the relationship thrive and develop in the long term?

Lunar Return

The Lunar Return analysis, also known as Lunar Return Analysis, is an astrological tool that focuses on the position of the Moon at its return to the exact position in an individual’s natal chart. Essentially, this analysis looks at the moment when the Moon is again precisely at the point in the zodiac where it was at the time of a person’s birth. This analysis aims to provide insights into the energies and themes relevant for the upcoming month.

In a Lunar Return analysis, various factors are considered, including:

  • House Positioning: The Moon returns to a specific house in the natal chart, which can indicate the areas of life that will be particularly emphasized in the coming month.
  • Aspects to the Natal Chart: The aspects between the returning Moon and the planets in the natal chart can provide clues about how the emotional and inner states of the individual will manifest during that month.
  • Transits: The transitory aspects of the Moon to other planets in the current horoscope are also examined to gain additional insights.
An overview of the month can be created by analyzing the above factors. This involves considering each lunar return throughout the month to get a comprehensive idea of how the energy flows will be for the individual during that period. This includes looking at the main themes, challenges, and opportunities that may arise throughout the month.

A Lunar Return analysis can answer various questions, including:

  • Which areas of life are in focus this month?
  • How can certain emotional states and needs best be addressed during this period?
  • What challenges or obstacles may arise this month, and how can they be managed?
  • What opportunities or possibilities are available in various areas of life?
